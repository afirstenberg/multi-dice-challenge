Multi Dice Championship Privacy Policy

Summary: We don't want your data. Please don't give it to us.

Multi Dice Championship is for fun and education only.

Anything you tell us may end up in our logs, but we're not asking for anything. We use Google tools to help track how many times you've visited and other usage metrics, but that is just to get some idea of how many people don't care - we don't use them, and we can't track this info to your account.

Some information such as a unique ID, your high score, and the number of times you have played are attached to your account as "stored data". You are able to view and clear this data at any time by visiting the Assistant Directory entry for Multi Dice Championship and selecting "View Stored Data" at the bottom of the page.

Updates to this policy will be posted here and on github.
A history of changes are available at github.
This policy was last updated on 11 Aug 2019.
